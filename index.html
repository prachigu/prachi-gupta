I'm a journalist based in New York City. According to Bill O'Reilly, I write for morons, and Donald Trump says I'm a "non-intelligent reporter." My book about Congresswoman Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez is available here via Workman Publishing.

Most recently, I was a senior reporter at Jezebel and co-host of Jezebel's former politics podcast, Big Time Dicks. I covered the 2016 election for Cosmopolitan.com, where I interviewed Ivanka Trump on her paid leave policy proposal, interviewed former First Lady Michelle Obama on her first solo trip to the Middle East, and traveled to Jordan to report on the refugee crisis. I have also written for Salon, IndieWire, Gawker, and Deadspin.

My work has been covered by outlets like the Columbia Journalism Review, Vox.com, Adweek, and featured in a Channel 4 Documentary. In October 2017, I hosted the New York Immigration Coalition's 30th Anniversary Gala honoring former US Attorney Preet Bharara and UNITE HERE's Maria Elena Durazo.
