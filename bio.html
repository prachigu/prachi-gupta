I'm a journalist and comedy writer. According to Bill O'Reilly, I "write for morons."

My work has been published in Cosmopolitan.com, Salon, HelloGiggles, IndieWire, Gawker, ANIMALNewYork, Someecards and CollegeHumor. I write about race, feminism, pop culture and politics. In 2014, Mediaite named me one of the "absolute best names in media" mostly because they agree that "Prachi" is a super cool name.

Prior to my writing career, I was a technology analyst at a large management consulting firm. I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh in 2009 with degrees in finance and English writing.

My hobbies include running, painting and looking up movie references because I never get them.
